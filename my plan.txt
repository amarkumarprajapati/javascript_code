1. Basic Arithmetic Operations
   Addition (+)
   Subtraction (-)
   Multiplication (*)
   Division (/)
   Modulo (%) (Remainder after division)
   Exponentiation (^ or pow(a, b))
2. Bitwise Operations (Used in bit manipulation)
AND (&)
OR (|)
XOR (^)
NOT (~)
Left Shift (<<)
Right Shift (>>)
3. Logarithmic Operations
Logarithm (log(n)) (Base 2, 10, or natural log)
Exponential Growth (e^x)
4. Modular Arithmetic (Used in cryptography, combinatorics)
Modular Addition: (a + b) % mod
Modular Multiplication: (a * b) % mod
Modular Inverse: a^(-1) % mod using Fermat’s theorem
5. Combinatorial Operations
Factorial (n!)
Permutations (P(n, r) = n! / (n-r)!)
Combinations (C(n, r) = n! / (r! * (n-r)!))
Binomial Coefficients (C(n, k) mod p)
6. Number Theory Operations
Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) (gcd(a, b))
Least Common Multiple (LCM) (lcm(a, b))
Prime Factorization
Sieve of Eratosthenes (finding prime numbers efficiently)
7. Graph Theory Math
Graph adjacency matrix operations
Matrix exponentiation (for shortest paths, Fibonacci, etc.)
Dijkstra’s algorithm (min-heap operations)
8. Probability and Statistics Operations
Expected Value (E(X))
Variance and Standard Deviation
Probability Calculations (Bayes’ theorem, Markov chains, etc.)
